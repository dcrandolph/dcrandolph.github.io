When phone and television and computer  
And heart  
Are bad news machines

Is life a matter of homeopathic reckoning  
Where the aim is to microdose a little cruelty  
A little ugliness  
A little pain  
To build immunity to a cruel ugly painful world?

I think it's more like a garden  
Where small things  
Become everything  
When loved.
