What needs to happen  
In order for it to be okay  
For you to live?  
How much money  
Or acclaim  
Or pain?  
How many likes  
Before you deserve  
Happiness?

Do trees deserve the rain?

What will it take to win?  
What does it mean to be right?

Does the moon earn the sunlight  
That we in turn earn each night?

Nothing works that way.  
Lies are exhausting.

If it is all just _one_  
Maybe trying to win  
is stepping outside the unity  
Which is an automatic loss.

If it is all just _love_  
Maybe trying to be right  
Is wrong.

I know you are tired  
But you do not have to be.  
You are not meant to be.  
That's why it feels so wrong  
To work so hard  
At something that gives you no pride.

It does not have to be this way.  
It is not meant to be this way.

Repeat after me:  
**I have decided**  
**To enjoy**  
**My life**.

You know  
What happens  
When you play  
By their rules.

You have been doing it  
For so long.  
So long.

What happens  
If you let go of rules  
And just play?

Remember?

Of course there is work to do.  
Enjoy it.  
You are here to grow.  
You have to grow.  
You are meant to grow.

Does it grow you  
Even if there is growing pain?  
Enjoy it.

If not,  
Say it again  
And make a choice.
