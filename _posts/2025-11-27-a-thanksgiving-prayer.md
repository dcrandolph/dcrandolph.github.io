title: a thanksgiving prayer.
date: 2025-11-27
tags: poetry prayer thanksgiving

We give thanks for this table, this home, this family.

We give thanks for all that sustains us.

We give thanks for this food. May we be fed, and may we feed others.

We welcome in the Teacher, we welcome in the Dreamer, we welcome in the Mystery.

May we see them in one another. May we see them in ourselves. May we see them in all things.

And so it is.
